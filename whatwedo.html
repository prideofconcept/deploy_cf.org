EDUCATION | HEALTH | AGRICULTURE

Education


Cummings Africa Foundation believes that education is the most powerful and effective catalyst to create a better future. Our approach is to partner with local and international institutions to create safe learning environments, strengthen teacher quality, enhance classroom learning, and train the next generation of leaders.

Health


We are committed to sustainable, quality healthcare for rural Africa, where there is an urgent need for basic infrastructure and critical necessities such as clean water, access to medical facilities and sanitation systems. Through partnerships and local ownership, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of the people of Africa.

Agriculture

With nearly one billion people, Africa still struggles to feed itself. Hunger kills more people each year than AIDS, TB and malaria combined. Cummings Africa Foundation believes that Africa has the power to feed itself and that smallholder farmers hold the key to sustained poverty reduction.
